# Project Objectives

## Summary
The "Rookies" workspace acts as a comprehensive, centralized software to eliminate tedious operations typical in small or micro-businesses. By merging WhatsApp usability with robust system-of-record mechanics, the platform streamlines the operational chaos often experienced by these businesses.

## Problem Statement
Small-to-medium business owners in India currently process sales manually via cluttered platforms (WhatsApp, Excel, notebooks). This introduces:
1. **Human Error:** Mistakes in transcription between chat messaging and order fulfillment tables.
2. **Missing Analytics:** Because the operations aren't centralized in standard models, the business lacks vital insights across payments, inventory churn, and customer trends.
3. **Scaling Restraints:** The mental and time overhead limits owners severely from acquiring more sales efficiently.

## The Rookies Solution
Rookies offers a **WhatsApp-first** data assimilation and synchronization engine masked with a highly simplistic dashboard. 

## Goals
- **Minimize Operational Fiction:** Enable businesses to parse conversational workflows (WhatsApp) immediately into standard database transactions (Order, Invoice, Contact data). 
- **Democratize Tech:** Offer a dashboard UI that looks nothing like traditional rigid ERP systems; it must be completely user-friendly for non-technical users.
- **Provide Actionable Intelligence:** Generate automated feedback regarding customer frequency mapping, top-selling inventory states, and missing payment trails directly within the workspace.

## Use Cases
- A **Home Baker** wants to collect orders via Instagram DMs and WhatsApp securely processing them without checking multiple logbooks.
- A **Kirana Store Owner** requires real-time feedback on top-selling SKUs based directly on aggregated invoices generated via chat checkouts.
- **Staff Users** assigned a "viewer" role checking pending daily order fulfillment states inside the warehouse.
